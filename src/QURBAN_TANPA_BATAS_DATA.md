WHAT IS QURBAN TANPA BATAS?
---------------------------

Qurban Tanpa Batas is an Islamic sacrifice (Qurban/Kurban) program that 
facilitates Indonesian Muslims in performing their religious obligation of 
Qurban during Eid al-Adha.

MISSION STATEMENT:
"Program 'Qurban Tanpa Batas' tidak hanya memfasilitasi Anda dalam menunaikan 
Ibadah pada Allah SWT, namun juga membuka peluang untuk berbagi kebaikan ke 
masyarakat luas yang mungkin jarang bahkan tidak pernah memakan daging Qurban."

English Translation:
"The 'Qurban Tanpa Batas' program not only facilitates you in performing worship 
to Allah SWT, but also opens opportunities to share goodness with the wider 
community who may rarely or even never eat Qurban meat."

ORGANIZATION DESCRIPTION:
A qurban program that makes it easy for Muslims to perform qurban worship easily, 
economically, and empowers local farmers.

VISION:
To become the most trusted and accessible online qurban platform in Indonesia, 
empowering local farmers while serving communities in need.

Translation:
The basic ruling of transactions is PERMISSIBLE according to the usul principle:

dirincikan saat online tidak sesuai, maka ini disebut qhoror, oleh karena itu 
Rosulullah shalla 'alaihi wa salam melarang jual beli gharar:"

Translation:
Qurban animals that are traded must match what has been detailed beforehand

The Messenger of Allah (peace be upon him) slaughtered qurban animals in an

"Hadits ini tidaklah menunjukkan wajib penyembelih sendiri akan tetapi sunnah

This hadith does NOT indicate that slaughtering oneself is OBLIGATORY, but

"Menukil pendapat Syekh Abu Bakar Jabir dalam kitab minhajul muslim bab udhhiyah

dengan diwakilkan oleh seseorang atau lembaga yang telah diamanahnya. Tidak ada 
perbedaan di kalangan ahli ilmu."

Translation:
Quoting the opinion of Sheikh Abu Bakar Jabir in the book Minhajul Muslim, 
chapter on sacrifice, that scholars are of the opinion that it is PERMISSIBLE 
to perform qurban WITHOUT witnessing and by DELEGATING to a person or institution 
that has been entrusted. There is NO DIFFERENCE among scholars on this matter.

OFFICIAL RULING:
"Pewakilan dalam penyembelihan hewan kurban disunnahkan sesorang muslim untuk 
menyembelih langsung dengan sendirinya, dan bila diwakilkan kepada orang lain 
untuk menyembelih kurbannya maka hukumnya diperbolehkan tanpa dosa dan diantara 
ahlu ilmi hal ini tidak ada perbedaan"

Translation:
Delegation in slaughtering qurban animals - it is recommended (sunnah) for a 
Muslim to slaughter directly themselves, and if delegated to someone else to 
slaughter their qurban, then the ruling is PERMISSIBLE WITHOUT SIN and among 
scholars there is NO DIFFERENCE on this matter.

SOURCE: Minhajul Muslim, Section 14 on Udhhiyyah and Aqiqah, page 266
